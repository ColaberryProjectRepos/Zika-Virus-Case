Measures Calculations

-       MoM Death =    VAR __PREV_Month = CALCULATE ([Death per Month], DATEADD('Date'[Date], -1, MONTH))

                 RETURN

                        DIVIDE ([Death per Month] - __PREV_Month, __PREV_Month) +0


                            Note  

                                 -Click on the measure and Go to Formatting Change to a                           percentage and round to 0 Decimal Place
